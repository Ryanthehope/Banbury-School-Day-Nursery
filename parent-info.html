A Typical Day At Nursery

Children benefit from a balance of familiar routines, play, rest and outdoor time across the day. A typical day may include:

1

Arrival and settling in

Children are welcomed into the room and supported to settle into the day.

2

Morning play and activities

Planned and child-led activities help build confidence, language and curiosity.

3

Snack and lunch

Mealtimes are part of the nursery routine and support independence and social skills.

4

Outdoor play

Children enjoy fresh air, movement and exploration in our enclosed outdoor areas.

5

Rest and quiet time

Quiet activities and rest are built around each child’s age and individual needs.

6

Stories, songs and home time

The day ends with familiar routines and handovers to families.
