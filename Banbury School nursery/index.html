Welcome to Banbury School Day Nursery

We have been providing high quality childcare in Banbury for over 30 years. Our nursery is situated in the grounds of Wykham Park Academy and offers a happy, safe and caring environment where children can grow, develop and thrive.

We are open from 8:00am to 5:30pm all year round and can cater for up to 80 children per day across our three purpose-built rooms.

Our Rooms

Each room is designed around the developmental needs of the children in it, staffed by experienced and qualified practitioners.

Star Room

For babies and toddlers aged 9 months to 18 months. A calm, nurturing space focused on attachment and early development.

Moon Room

For children aged 18 months to 2½ years. Focused on language development, independence and creative exploration.

Sun Room

Our pre-school room for children aged 2½ to 5 years. Preparing children for school through structured play and the EYFS curriculum.
